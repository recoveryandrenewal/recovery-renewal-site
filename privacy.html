Our Commitment

Recovery & Renewal is built on a simple principle: your recovery journey is yours alone. We don't collect your data. We don't track your behavior. We don't monetize your information. Period.

This app will always be free. No paywalls. No subscriptions. No "premium" features. No ads.

What We DON'T Collect

  • No user accounts or registration
  • No email addresses or phone numbers
  • No location tracking or GPS data
  • No analytics or usage statistics
  • No third-party SDKs, trackers, or data brokers

"If you're using Recovery & Renewal, we genuinely have no idea. That's by design."

On Your Device

All readings, prayers, milestone dates, and step work are stored locally using your device's secure storage. Nothing leaves your phone.

Offline First

The entire app works without an internet connection. 31,103 Bible verses and 2,400+ devotionals are pre-loaded.

Open Source Transparency

You don't have to take our word for it. Our complete source code is available on GitHub. Anyone can audit our code to verify zero data collection.

View Source Code

Deleting Your Data

When you uninstall Recovery & Renewal, all your local data is deleted with it. We don't have backups, copies, or records—because we never collected anything in the first place.

GDPR / CCPA

Since we collect no personal data, data protection laws like GDPR and CCPA don't apply to our app's operation. You have nothing to request, delete, or opt out of—because there is nothing to request.
